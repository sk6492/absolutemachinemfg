About Us

Our Story

Absolute Machine Mfg. Inc.  is a highly experienced manufacturer of small and large industrial parts and custom CNC machining. We are located in Mississauga, Ontario, and serve a customer base spanning a wide variety of industries in the Greater Toronto Area. We offer complete manufacturing solutions focused on quality, delivery, and competitive pricing. We are able to machine a wide variety of materials such as steel, stainless steel, tool steel, aluminum, copper, brass, bronze, rubber, and plastics.

Our Approach

We practice lean methodologies with modern equipment in order to bring innovative solutions to its customers. We aim to earn our customers’ trust and build partnerships by meeting their unique needs. We thrive on challenging projects and strive for excellence.
