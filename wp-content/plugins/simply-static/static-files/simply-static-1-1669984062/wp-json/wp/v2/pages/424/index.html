CNC Programmer\\Machinist
\n<\/strong>(October 3, 2022)
\n<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Absolute Machine Manufacturing is looking for an experienced CNC Programmer\\Machinist to help out in our busy new shop.<\/p>\n

Job Requirements<\/strong><\/p>\n

    \n
  • Must know programming in Fusion 360 .CAM 2D and 3D for vertical mills and lathes.<\/li>\n
  • Knowledge of tooling.<\/li>\n
  • Must have 3 years of experience in similar role.<\/li>\n
  • Knowledge of tooling for machining different grades of ferrous and nonferrous material.<\/li>\n
  • Must be able to program and setup multiple CNC\u2019s.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    You Should Be:<\/strong><\/p>\n

      \n
    • Able to work with minimal supervision, however, we encourage you to ask questions<\/li>\n
    • A very hard worker with sensitivity to meet tight customer order deadlines<\/li>\n
    • Able to run machines within a few days.\u00a0 We will train you and encourage you to observe and ask but expect\u00a0 you to be able to comfortably and accurately setup and run parts within 1-2 days.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      Starting Pay rate is $26\/hr.
      \nGreat opportunity to excel based on performance.<\/p>\n

      E-Mail your resume with ‘Application: CNC Programmer’ in the subject line to info@absolutemachinemfg.com.<\/p>\n
